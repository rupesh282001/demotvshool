OUR ACADEMIC RULES

RULES

  • High level of discipline must be followed.
  • Punctuality and regularity are must in attendance.
  • Students who are absent the previous day should bring a note of report regarding the leave, in the case of illness leave letter is mandatory.
  • Student must have 75% of attendance, if the student does not have 75% of attendance is not eligible for the examination.
  • Students should enter the school campus on time, as per the school schedule.
  • Students must wear neat and laundered uniforms with perfect accessories of socks, shoes, tie, belt, ribbons. Hair should be neatly combed and tied up.
  • Every student is required to carry the school identity card each day of the school.
  • No student is permitted to leave school during school hours without a valid permission slip from the class teacher/ principal.
  • Students are not allowed to receive private tuitions from the teachers of the school.

CODE OF BEHAVIOUR

  • Students must report any incident of bullying and ragging to the teachers immediately.
  • Students will not use foul or offensive language.
  • Students must report to class on time after break or activity periods.
  • No student is allowed to engage in ragging, either on or off campus.

PROHIBITIONS

  • Students are not allowed to use gadgets inside the campus.
  • Students are not allowed to defecate in or outside the school premises.
  • Students are not permitted to bring expensive electronics or other technological gadgets to school. If they do, the administration will take stern action.
  • Students are not permitted to wear expensive jewellery, watches and other accessories.
  • Students are cautioned to avoid consuming food from street sellers.
  • Smoking in the campus is strictly prohibited.

TRANSPORT

  • Damage done even by accident or mistake should be reported immediately to the class teacher, class captain or to the Principal/Vice-Principal/ Headmaster/Headmistress.
  • Protect and safeguard school properties.
  • Strict discipline must be maintained in the school bus. Misbehaviour or any damage to the school transport can lead to withdrawal of bus facilities.
  • Keeping the school buildings and grounds clean and tidy, moving about in a quiet and orderly manner.
  • Bus service issues or changes to bus routes must be brought to the attention of the school administration.
