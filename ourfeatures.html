FEATURES OF OUR SCHOOL

  • Classes Nursery to Grade 9 in co-educational system.
  • Affiliated with Central Board of Secondary Education.
  • Qualified and motivated teachers with perfect blend of knowledge, quality, innovation and compassion.
  • Digital facilities to enable the students to learn well and face the challenges of the ambitious world.
  • Creates awareness of the rich heritage and culture of our country among students.
  • Outdoor training programs and adventure camps for students to generate confidence and dynamism in their personality to face the challenges.
  • Pager method will be followed for assembly and for information purpose.
  • Special assemblies are also organized with cultural activities on special occasions.
  • Maintains well-furnished & centrally air-conditioned library which accommodates more than 50 users at a time and has around 1500 books and magazines.
  • Well-equipped laboratories viz. Science Lab, Computer Science Lab, Mathematics Lab, Language Lab etc.
  • Every student will be provided the opportunity to participate in various national competitive exams or competitions for their exposure to the greater competitive world.
  • Personalized guidance and counselling.
  • Digital classroom facility which initiates smart table where students can learn through the digital screen.
  • Our school premises are well protected under CCTV surveillance.
  • The curriculum is rigorous, collaborated with Oxford, and varies according to the grade. Teacher performance is regularly monitored by the management. Both teachers and students strive for excellence in academic and extracurricular activities.
  • A commitment to excellence with a focus on holistic development, enabling students to understand real-life applications.
  • Best performance and a student-centered approach to teaching will be recognized and rewarded.
  • Transport facilities are available with live GPS tracking, enabling effective communication with parents and the management. A female caretaker will be present during transit to ensure safety and security.

ROBOTICS LAB

  • The high-tech robotics lab will give students an opportunity to make science fiction a reality. The students will be taught to construct and assemble computerized robots in a graded fashion.
  • After learning the basic assembly of the components of the robot, they will learn to make complex functioning advanced robots.
  • This gives them a hands-on experience in the world of artificial intelligence.

Hands On Learning Through Modern Labs

  • At our institution, we provide well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Computer, and Mathematics Laboratories designed to offer hands-on learning experiences that bridge theory with real-world application. Each lab is furnished with modern instruments, advanced technology, and interactive learning tools to foster experimentation, innovation, and critical thinking among students.
